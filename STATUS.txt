📊 HOW TEMPLATE ROTATION WORKS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Without Rotation (Bad):
  Email 1 → Same Content → SPAM ✗
  Email 2 → Same Content → SPAM ✗
  Email 3 → Same Content → SPAM ✗

With Rotation (Good):
  Email 1 → Template A → ✓
  Email 2 → Template B → ✓
  Email 3 → Template C → ✓
  Email 4 → Template A → ✓ (cycles)

Result: Better deliverability, less spam detection
